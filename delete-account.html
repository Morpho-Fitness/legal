Delete Your Account & Data

Morpho: AI Fitness Coach · Account & data deletion

You can delete your Morpho account and all associated data at any time, directly in the app or by emailing us. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Delete your account from the app

  1. Open Morpho and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Help & legal → Delete account.
  3. Type DELETE to confirm, then tap Delete account.

Your account and the data tied to it are removed immediately and irreversibly.

Request deletion without the app

If you can't access the app, email brohvolodimir@gmail.com from the email address on your account and ask us to delete it. We will verify ownership and complete the deletion within 30 days.

What is deleted

Deleting your account permanently erases all data we hold for you, including:

  • Your profile, body metrics, and goals
  • All generated training and nutrition plans
  • All meals you've logged or scanned, and meal/progress photos
  • Progress entries, measurements, and AI insights
  • Health & fitness data imported from Apple Health / Health Connect and connected wearables
  • Medication, hydration, cycle, and daily check-in entries
  • AI coach chat history
  • Your authentication record (sign-in credentials)

What may be retained

  • Billing records: subscription and purchase records are held by Google Play and our payments processor (RevenueCat) where required for tax, accounting, and legal obligations. Morpho does not control these retention periods.
  • Anonymized/aggregated data that can no longer be linked to you may be retained for analytics.
  • Limited records we are required to keep by law.

Subscriptions are separate

Deleting your account does not cancel an active subscription. Google bills subscriptions separately — cancel from Play Store → Subscriptions before deleting your account to avoid further charges.
