Privacy Policy

Morpho: AI Fitness Coach · Last updated: June 2026

Your data, your judgment. We encrypt everything at rest and in transit, and we never sell your data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the control you have.

What we collect

  • Account: your email and (optional) name when you sign up.
  • Body profile: goal, age, height, weight, training days, dietary preferences, equipment, and any injuries you choose to share.
  • Activity: workouts you start or complete, meals you log or scan, weight and measurement entries, and your subscription state.
  • Apple Health (optional): steps, sleep, heart rate, and active energy — read only after you grant permission in the iOS prompt, and only to tune your daily targets and insights.
  • Diagnostics: crash reports (via Sentry) and product-usage events (via PostHog), tied to your account so we can debug your issues. Both are opt-out in Settings → Privacy.

What we don't collect

  • We don't sell your data to advertisers, ever.
  • We don't track you across other apps or websites.
  • We don't access your contacts, location, or microphone.
  • We never sell or share your Apple Health data.

How we use what we collect

We use your profile and activity to generate your personalised training and nutrition plans, track your progress, and improve the app. Diagnostics help us find crashes and broken flows so we can fix them.

AI processing

When you scan a meal, log food by text, or run a body or form analysis, the relevant content — your meal/body photos, the text you enter, and basic profile stats (age, sex, height, weight, goals) — is sent to OpenAI to generate the result. It is processed only to return your analysis and is not used to train their models. We never send your name, your medication names, or your contact details.

Who processes your data

  • Supabase — your account and content (hosted Postgres), encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • Sentry & PostHog — diagnostics and usage analytics, under our organisation.
  • RevenueCat and the Apple App Store — subscription state and billing.
  • OpenAI — AI analysis requests, as described above.

Data retention & your rights

You can export or delete your account at any time from Settings → Privacy → Delete Account. Deletion removes all rows we hold for you immediately and is irreversible. You can email us to request a full data export, ask questions, or report a privacy concern.

Children

Morpho is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16.

Not medical advice

Morpho is a fitness, nutrition, habit, and education app. We do not diagnose, treat, prescribe medication, or provide medical advice. Speak with a licensed clinician before making health decisions.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes are announced in-app, and the "last updated" date above will change.
