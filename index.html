What you get

One app, your whole training life.

Everything works together — your plan, your nutrition, and your progress, all tuned to you and re-tuned as you change.

Personalised plans

A training plan from your goal, experience, days, and gear — plus daily calorie and macro targets that match it.

Scan a meal, skip the math

Point your camera at a meal for instant calorie and macro estimates. Log by photo or text — correct the AI in a tap.

An AI coach in your pocket

Ask anything about training, nutrition, and recovery. Get clear, grounded guidance — educational, never medical advice.

See real progress

Weight, measurements, and progress photos over time — with AI body and form analysis when you want a closer look.

Works with Apple Health

Optionally reads steps, sleep, heart rate, and active energy to fine-tune your targets. Always opt-in.

Private by design

We never sell your data and never track you across other apps. Export or delete everything anytime, in-app.
